## Activity listing

* **Displays:**
    * list of activity (`ul.listing.activity`)
    * items sorted from newest to oldest by date
    * 15 items per page
* **Visible to:** everyone (public, users, and team members)

### Items

Each item displays:

* Activity description
* Date of activity (relative to now)
